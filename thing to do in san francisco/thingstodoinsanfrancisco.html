Exploratorium:

Exploratorium
5 stars - "My Review" Jonathan Hollander Location is near Pier 39, so you might as well Check that out too. Awesome if you love science!!

The Exploratorium is a public learning laboratory in San Francisco exploring the world through science, art, and human perception. Its mission is to create inquiry-based experiences that transform learning worldwide. It has been described by the New York Times as the most important science museum to have opened since the mid-20th century.Photo By Victor R. Ruiz

San Francisco Ferry Building:

Ferry Building
4.2 stars - "My Review" Jonathan Hollander The Farmer's Market is pretty fun here. Great Restaurants nearby, and really close to Pier 39.

Twin Peaks:

Twin Peaks
5 stars - "My Review" Jonathan Hollander Whether you go during the day or night, It's Amaaazing. Go on a clear day!
